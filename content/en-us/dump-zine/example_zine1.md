### 0 - A curious mind who likes hacking

Sup people!

It's been a while since I submitted content for the folks who like hacking. 
The funny thing is that right before starting this article I was reading phrack #71... 
and man, it's so good to see that hacking is alive and watching you.

Hacking awakens many feelings: the need to learn, to understand how things work. 
Hacking teaches you to be a better person. From it can arise the teacher, the hostile, 
the defender of an idea, whatever... hacking transforms you.

The idea of this zine is exactly to rescue this essence. 
To bring to paper (or to the black screen) that feeling of discovery and sharing 
that moved the scene back in the days. Hope you enjoy reading it as much as I enjoyed writing it.
